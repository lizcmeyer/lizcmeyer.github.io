About

Peter graduated with a degree in Fine Arts from Franklin Pierce University in Rindge, New Hampshire, and has been a practicing potter for the last eight years. In addition to a desire for perfection in each piece, his goal is to make handcrafted pottery an affordable luxury for every ceramics lover.

Process

Drawing from Korean and British pottery design inspiration and with an admiration for potters Hans Coper and Walter Keeler, Peter's work is uniquely made for everyday, utilitarian use.
